## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **VoiceGuard**, your AI-powered defense against voice phishing (vishing) and deepfake audio scams. This platform is designed to help you analyze suspicious audio recordings and determine whether they feature a genuine human voice or an AI-generated clone.

This guideline will walk you through the essential features of VoiceGuard to ensure you can protect yourself and your loved ones from digital fraud.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Open the VoiceGuard web application and click on **Sign Up** on the homepage.
2. Enter your full name, email address, and a strong password. (Alternatively, you can sign in using your Google account).
3. Check your email inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.

### Enabling Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
To ensure maximum security for your account:
1. Log in and navigate to your **Profile / Settings**.
2. Click on **Enable MFA**.
3. Use an authenticator app (such as Google Authenticator or Authy) to scan the provided QR code.
4. Enter the 6-digit code generated by the app to confirm setup.

## 3. Deepfake Audio Detection

The core feature of VoiceGuard is the AI Detection Engine, which analyzes audio for microscopic synthetic artefacts.

### How to Upload an Audio File
1. From your dashboard, navigate to the **Detect** page.
2. Drag and drop your suspicious audio file into the upload area, or click to browse files on your device.
3. Wait a few seconds for the AI engine to process the file.

### Supported Formats
VoiceGuard supports the following standard audio formats:
* `.WAV`
* `.MP3`
* `.FLAC`
* `.M4A`
*(Note: For best results, audio clips should be between 3 and 30 seconds long and have minimal background noise.)*

### Interpreting the Results
Once processing is complete, you will receive a confidence score and a verdict:
* 🟢 **Genuine / Real:** The voice contains natural human vocal patterns.
* 🔴 **Deepfake / AI-Generated:** The voice contains synthetic characteristics indicative of AI cloning or generation.
